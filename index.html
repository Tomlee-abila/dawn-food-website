About Us

Celebrating Cultural Diversity and Natural Environment

Welcome to Hot Dish Restaurant, a culinary destination that celebrates the vibrant cultural diversity and stunning natural environment of Nairobi. Nestled in the suburb of Karen, our establishment combines the best of both worlds to offer you a unique dining experience.

Exquisite Cuisine with Skilled Technique

At Hot Dish, we take pride in our ingredient-led contemporary cuisine. Our highly skilled team, led by Chef Martin, showcases their expertise and drive for perfection in every dish we serve. From innovative techniques to carefully selected ingredients, our cuisine reflects our commitment to culinary excellence.

Glamorous Yet Welcoming Atmosphere

Step into the world of Hot Dish and experience a perfect balance of glamour and warmth. As a four-time winner of the Nairobi magazine Pulse Awards for Best Upscale Restaurant, we provide a sophisticated and elegant ambiance. However, we also ensure that our guests feel relaxed and welcomed, thanks to our attentive and friendly services.

Located in Karen, A Suburban Retreat

Hot Dish Restaurant is conveniently located in Karen, a serene and picturesque suburb of Nairobi. This tranquil setting offers a peaceful escape from the city's hustle and bustle, allowing you to enjoy your dining experience in a calm and inviting environment.

Catering to Every Dining Occasion

Whether you're seeking a delightful breakfast, a leisurely brunch, a quick lunch, or an unforgettable dinner, Hot Dish has you covered. Our extensive menu is designed to cater to various dining occasions, ensuring that there's something for everyone. Additionally, we offer a bar dining menu for those looking to unwind with after-work drinks or casual get-togethers.

Creating Memorable Moments

Hot Dish is not just a restaurant; it's a place where memories are made. We specialize in hosting formal gala events, adding a touch of culinary excellence to your special occasions. Whether it's a corporate gathering, a milestone celebration, or a romantic dinner, our dedicated team will ensure that your event is nothing short of extraordinary.

Experience the celebration of Nairobi's cultural diversity, the flavors of our exquisite cuisine, and the warm hospitality of Hot Dish Restaurant. Join us today for a remarkable dining experience that will leave a lasting impression on your taste buds and in your heart.
